3. Create a webpage to define the following terms using definition list.

• Information poaching
The risk that in any contractual relationship, information that is transfered between parties for purposes specified in the contract.This could be done for its own economic benefit.

• Electronic commerce
Electronic commerce, commonly known as e-commerce or eCommerce, is a type of industry where the buying and selling of products or services is conducted over electronic systems such as the Internet and other computer networks.

• Cyber bullying
Cyber bullying is the use of the Internet and related technologies to harm or harass other people, in a deliberate, repeated, and hostile manner.

• Virus
VIRUS stands for Vital Information Resources Under Siege. A computer virus is a small software program that replicates itself at great speed and inferes with computer operation. It spreads from one system to another and results in the destruction of large amounts of information and data.
